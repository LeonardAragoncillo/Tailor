Aglaonema Katrina

Aglaonema ‘Katrina’ is a specific cultivar of the Aglaonema plant known for its striking and colorful foliage. This variety is highly sought after for its unique leaf pattern and vibrant colors. The leaves of Aglaonema ‘Katrina’ are typically a rich green with a mix of silver-gray markings and splashes of bright pink or red. The contrasting colors and patterns create a visually appealing display that adds a pop of color to any indoor space. Like other Aglaonema varieties, ‘Katrina’ is a relatively low-maintenance plant that can tolerate low to moderate light conditions, making it suitable for indoor environments with limited natural light. It prefers regular watering, allowing the soil to slightly dry out between waterings. With its eye-catching foliage and easy care requirements, Aglaonema ‘Katrina’ brings a touch of vibrancy and tropical beauty to homes, offices, and indoor gardens.
